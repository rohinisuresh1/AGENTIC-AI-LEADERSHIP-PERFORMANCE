# AGENTIC-AI-LEADERSHIP-PERFORMANCE

This project presents a data-driven analytical study on the impact of Agentic AI on organizational leadership and performance, built using Power BI for interactive dashboard visualization and powered by the Agentic AI Leadership Dataset sourced from Kaggle. As organizations across industries increasingly turn to advanced AI systems for strategic decision-making, understanding how Agentic AI — capable of autonomous action and dynamic adaptation — influences business outcomes has become a critical area of research.

Unlike traditional AI systems, Agentic AI operates with a degree of autonomy that allows it to respond to changing environments, support leadership-level decisions, and drive measurable improvements in productivity and task efficiency. This project dives deep into how factors such as trust, maturity level, adoption success, and industry type interact to shape organizational performance in an AI-integrated environment.

The dataset encompasses a carefully curated set of criteria including Trust Level, Industry, Performance Category, Adoption Success, Productivity Improvement (%), Task Success Rate (%), AI Maturity Level, and Response Time. These dimensions collectively provide a holistic view of where AI-driven leadership is thriving and where gaps in adoption and effectiveness remain across sectors.

The Power BI dashboard translates these complex variables into clear, actionable visual insights — enabling stakeholders to identify patterns between AI adoption rates and performance outcomes, evaluate productivity gains tied to successful AI implementation, and assess how task success rates vary by industry and maturity level. The dashboard is designed to support better organizational decision-making rather than just surface-level reporting.
Key findings from the analysis confirm that organizations with higher trust in AI and successful adoption strategies consistently achieve superior productivity and task success outcomes. The research also reveals that AI's impact is not uniform — it varies significantly by industry, underlining the importance of context-aware implementation strategies.

The project further recommends actionable strategies such as feedback-driven AI improvement loops, real-time data monitoring, regular AI audits, predictive analytics integration, and robust data privacy frameworks to ensure responsible and effective AI deployment.
This repository serves as a practical reference for data analysts, business leaders, and AI researchers exploring the measurable effects of agentic systems on modern organizational performance and leadership effectiveness.
